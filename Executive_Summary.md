## Executive Overview
### The risks to the stability of liquid capital and availability of consumer credit from uncontrolled customer attrition in systemically vital retail financial institutions are apparent. This summary outlines the successful implementation of a sophisticated behavioural analytics panel for the evaluation of a regional banking portfolio ($N = 10,000$). This has allowed us to identify structural operational vulnerabilities by combining backend feature engineering with live streamlit interface panels, and to make two clear data driven interventions to protect the core assets of the bank.

Key Performance Benchmarks Established
| Strategic Indicator | Operational Definition | Metric Findings & Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Engagement Retention Ratio** | Active Member Churn vs. Inactive Member Churn | Proves that active account usage cuts baseline attrition in half across all core demographics. |

| **High-Asset Disengagement** | Count of high-asset accounts showing zero active engagement | Flags "Ghost Accounts" holding significant assets that are completely stagnant and at risk of leaving. |
| **Relationship Strength (RSI)** | Composite behavioral score (Scale: 1–9) | Proves that an **RSI score of 5.00** acts as the threshold where customer loyalty stabilizes. |

# Critical Insights for Stakeholders
## 1. The Multi-Product Vulnerability:
### The analytics system caught an alarming risk curve. Expanding a customer's portfolio to 3 or 4 products results in a turnover rate near 100%. Pushing too many products too fast creates massive customer friction rather than increasing loyalty.
## 2. The RSI Stabilization Line:
### A calculated RSI(Relationship Strength Index) score of 5 serves as the reliable dividing line for account safety. Customers scoring below 5 represent a highly volatile risk group, while those at 5 or above show excellent long-term retention metrics.
# Government & Regulatory Action Plan
## Stabilize Systemic Liquidity:
### Mandate that retail banking operations shift their marketing focus from high-volume cross-selling to high-quality engagement, keeping customers anchored at the optimal 2-product safety tier.
## Deploy Proactive Retention Programs:
### Use the high-value disengaged customer detector built into the system to automatically generate retention target lists, allowing teams to secure large financial deposits before capital leaves the institution.
